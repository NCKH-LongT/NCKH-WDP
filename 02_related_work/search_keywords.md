# Search Keywords for Trend Tracking System

- "research topic evolution visualization"
- "scholarly big data analytics platform"

- **Query 1 (Google Scholar/IEEE):** 
  `("scientific publication" OR "scholarly data") AND ("trend tracking" OR "topic evolution") AND "visualization"`

  `("BERTopic" OR "LLM") AND "academic literature" AND "trend analysis"`

  `"openalex integration" AND "scholarly analytics platform"`
