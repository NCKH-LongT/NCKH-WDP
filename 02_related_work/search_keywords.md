# Boolean queries (examples)

## Scopus / Web of Science style
- ("smart parking" OR "parking management" OR "parking system") AND ("multi-level" OR "multi-storey" OR "multi-storey") AND (reservation OR allocation OR allocation algorithm OR optimization)
- ("parking occupancy" OR "occupancy detection" OR "parking detection") AND (camera OR "computer vision" OR CNN OR deep learning) AND (multi-level OR "multi-storey")

## IEEE Xplore style
- ("smart parking" OR "parking reservation") AND ("multi-level" OR "multi-storey") AND (allocation OR scheduler OR optimizer)
- ("parking occupancy" AND (camera OR vision OR "deep learning" OR YOLO OR CNN))

## Google Scholar (broad)
- "smart parking" "multi-level" reservation allocation
- "parking occupancy detection" "camera" "multi-level"

# Tips / variations
- Add dataset names to narrow results: AND (CNRPark OR PKLot OR "PK Lot" OR "UA-DETRAC")
- Add application/context: AND ("valet" OR "commercial parking" OR "airport" OR "mall")
- Use wildcards where allowed (e.g., reservation* for reservation, reservations, reservation-aware)
- Exclude irrelevant topics: NOT ("on-street" OR bicycle OR motorcycle) when searching multi-storey car park literature

# Search variants for supervisor/recommendation or user-facing features
- ("reservation" OR "booking") AND ("user interface" OR dashboard OR "user experience")
- ("license plate recognition" OR ALPR OR OCR) AND parking
